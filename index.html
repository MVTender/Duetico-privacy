Privacy Policy for Duetico

Effective date: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Duetico is a mobile application designed to help couples or individuals manage their finances, track expenses, set financial goals, and maintain a clear overview of their budgets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous device identifiers (Firebase UID)
  • Expenses and financial goals (amounts, categories, dates, who paid)
  • Partner profile information (name, avatar, color)
  • App settings (language, theme, currency)
  • Trial and subscription status

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to:

  • Synchronize information between devices of the same couple
  • Display statistics and charts
  • Manage trials and subscriptions

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database (Europe-west1) and locally on your device (SQLite). We use Firebase security rules and SSL encryption to protect your information. Only authorized devices within a couple’s space can access shared data.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not transmit your data to third parties for marketing or analytics. Firebase (Google) is used only as the backend storage and synchronization service.

6. Children’s Privacy

Duetico is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hayperion.it@gmail.com.
